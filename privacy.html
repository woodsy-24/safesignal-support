Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

SafeSignal (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when using the SafeSignal mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (if signing in with email)
  • Apple ID identifier (if using Sign in with Apple)

App Usage Data
We use Firebase Analytics to collect limited usage data such as:

  • App interactions
  • Device type
  • App version
  • General performance metrics
This data helps us improve app reliability and usability.

Check-In and Group Data
SafeSignal stores:

  • Check-in timestamps
  • Group membership information
  • Notification tokens required to send alerts

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No location tracking
  • No background movement monitoring
  • No contact list access
  • No data selling

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide check-in functionality
  • Send notifications to group members
  • Maintain account security
  • Improve app performance and reliability

Third-Party Services

SafeSignal uses Firebase services provided by Google, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
These services may process data in accordance with Google’s privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no system can guarantee complete security.
