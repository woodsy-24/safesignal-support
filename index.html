Frequently Asked Questions

Does SafeSignal track location?
No. SafeSignal does not use location tracking. It focuses on intentional check-ins.

Do all group members receive notifications?
All private group members that have opted-in for notifications and have them enabled will receive a notification.

Is my data shared?
SafeSignal does not sell or share personal data with third parties.
